What is IEEE?

Our Mission

The IEEE Computer Society is dedicated to advancing the theory, practice, and application of computer science and engineering technology. The Society promotes an active exchange of information, ideas, and technological innovation among its members, promoting educational initiatives in various fields related to computing and information technology.

Professional Standards

The Computer Society serves its members and the profession by promoting high professional standards among its members while supporting research, education, and industry collaboration. The Society is closely affiliated with IEEE, with a special focus on the Society's field of interest in computing and related disciplines.
